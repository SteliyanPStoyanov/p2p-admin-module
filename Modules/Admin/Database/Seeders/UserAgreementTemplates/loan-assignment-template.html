2. Subject and scope of the Assignment Agreement

Purpose of the current Agreement:

  • Afranga operates an online claim marketplace Platform allowing the electronic assignment of claims via which the Claim specified below shall be assigned.
  • The Loan Originator is a licensed/ registered lender, who is an owner of a Claim against a Borrower.
  • The User wishes to purchase Claim(s) from the Loan originators listed on Afranga’s Platform.

The parties understand and agree that:

2.1. This Assignment Agreement shall be concluded between the Assignee (the User), the Assignor (Loan Originator) and Afranga who operates the Platform facilitating the transactions.

2.2. According to the provisions of the current Assignment agreement, the Loan Originator agrees to assign to the User (in his capacity of Assignee) the Claim (or part of a Claim) against the Borrower arising from the Loan Agreement in exchange for a Claim Price.

2.3. The User (Assignee) confirms and understands that the Claims purchased on Afranga’s Platform from the Loan Originator are not all of the Loan Originator’s claims against the Borrower, and the User shall not become the sole creditor of the Borrower.

2.4. The User confirms and understands that Afranga and the Loan Originator shall manage the Claim together with the claims of other Users against the respective Borrower.


3. Conclusion of the Assignment Agreement

3.1. The User (Assignee) confirms that he is familiar with the terms of the current Agreement as well as with the terms of the Afranga User Agreement, General Terms and Conditions and understands and accepts the rights and obligations arising from the two documents.

3.2. The User (Assignee) confirms that he wishes to enter into the current Assignment Agreement.

3.3. The Claim will be transferred from the Loan originator and assigned to the User (Assignee) at the time when the Claim price is fully paid by the User.

3.4. The User (Assignee) declares that at the time of conclusion of the current Agreement, he is compliant with the prerequisites under Article 2.2 of the Afranga User Agreement, General Terms and Conditions.


4. Claim price

4.1. The User (Assignee) is obligated to pay the Loan Originator the Claim Price indicated in the current Assignment Agreement.

4.2. The price shall be paid by transferring the monetary equivalent of the Claim price from the funds available on the User’s Afranga Account to the Loan Originator’s account.

4.3. Once the Claim is considered transferred, Afranga will make sure to facilitate the transfer of funds from the User’s Afranga Account to the Loan Originator’s account.

5.2.5. Facilitating the loan repayment, interest, contractual penalties and/or interests as well as any other ancillary payments arising from the Loan Agreement.

5.2.6. Facilitating the buyback obligations of the Loan Originator, in case they are exercised.

5.2.7. Facilitating the transfer of the buyback price to the Assignee.

5.2.8. Collecting late payments in the interests of the User (Assignee) by the Loan originator and/ or Borrower in case the Loan Originator and/ or the Borrower fails to pay any amounts on their due date.

5.2.9. Establishing any collateral securing performance of the Loan Originator’s obligations arising from this Agreement.

5.2.10. Enforcing any security created for the benefit of the Assignee and handle all matters related to the performance of out-of-court actions for collection of claims and enforcement of security in the name of the Assignee and for the benefit of the Assignee.

5.2.11. Drafting amendments or signing additional annex agreements to collateral documents without a prior coordination with the Assignee.

5.2.12. Distributing among all the Creditors, who at the moment of receipt of funds have an effective claim against the Borrower arising out of the Loan Agreement, the funds received from the Loan originator or the Borrower for settling obligations under the Loan Agreement.

5.3. Afranga shall act on behalf of the Loan originator by:

5.3.1. Providing the Loan originator with the necessary documentation to transfer Claims, arising from a Loan Assignment Agreement as well as buyback of such Claims.

5.4. Afranga shall perform the actions it finds necessary and feasible to facilitate the timely and full recovery of payments from the Loan Originator or other party servicing the Claim without engaging the Assignee (User) with respect to the particular Claim.

7.2.3. Take all actions required relating to the repayment of the Loan and fulfilment of the Loan Agreement until the Loan is repaid in full and the Claim is discharged in full.

7.2.5. The Assignee authorizes the Loan originator to continue to service the Claim after its transfer. The Parties thus agree and approve that they shall not inform the Borrower of the conclusion of the current Assignment Agreement.

8. Borrower’s payments

8.1. The Borrower shall make the Borrower’s Payments to the Loan Originator pursuant to the Loan Agreement and the schedule to the Loan Agreement.

8.2. The Loan Originator shall immediately transfer all received Borrower’s Payments to Afranga.

8.3. After receiving the Borrower’s Payments, including the case where the Borrower makes an early repayment, fully or partially and the case where the Borrower performs only a partial payment of his obligations, the Loan Originator shall immediately transfer them to Afranga.

8.4. After Afranga has received the relevant share of the Borrower’s Payments, Afranga shall proportionally as per participation distribute the payments among all creditors having claims arising from the Loan Agreement against the Borrower.

8.5. Afranga is entitled before facilitating the distribution of the funds received from the Loan Originator (Assignor) to the User (Assignee) to deduct therefrom Claim service fee and other fees due according to its Price List.

8.6. The Assignee undertakes not to bring any claims against the Loan Originator, Afranga or the Borrower concerning full or partial early repayment of Loan based on the lost profit and any other losses.


9. Buyback of the Claim

9.1. The Loan originator shall be obliged to unilaterally exercise its buyback obligations if the Borrower delays the payments arising from the Loan Agreement by more than 60 (sixty) days.

9.2. The Loan originator hereby authorizes Afranga, in case if the Loan originator’s obligation to exercise its buyback obligations have occurred, to write off from the Loan Originator’s account and transfer the sums equivalent to the buyback price to the account of the Assignee immediately, without obtaining prior separate order from the Loan originator.

9.3. The Claim shall be considered as transferred back to the Loan originator from the moment of the payment of the sums equivalent to the buyback price in the Afranga Account of the Assignee.

9.4. The Assignee shall not make any complaints or claims against the Loan originator in respect of the exercise of the buyback obligations due to lost profit and any other damages in this respect.

9.5. In case the Loan Originator exercises the buyback obligations, the buyback price of the Claim paid by the Loan originator to the Assignee for the buyback is equal to the total amount of the remaining principal amount of the Claim the Assignee holds and accumulated and outstanding Interest of the Claim the Assignee holds specified in Afranga’s platform in euros (EUR) as at the time of exercising the buyback obligations.

9.6. The buyback price under this section forms a full and final payment to the Assignee for the re- transfer of the Claim.

The User (Assignee) understands and acknowledges that Afranga is entitled to receive a compensation of reasonable expenses incurred when acting as an authorized representative of the Users (Assignees) in the event of a Borrower default and agrees to therefore compensate Afranga for such costs.
